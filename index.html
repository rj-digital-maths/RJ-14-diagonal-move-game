HOW TO PLAY

  • 1.Objective: Invert the layout configuration by safely swapping the Left (L) and Right (R) fleets entirely.
  • 2.Select Token: Click on any active coin piece to target its vector paths.
  • 3.Valid Movement: Tokens move ONLY along open diagonal trajectories. Click highlighted cells to execute.
  • 4.Restrictions: No jumping over other tokens. Horizontal and vertical steps are prohibited.
  • 5.Move Economy: Balance paths wisely. Inversion must succeed within exactly 14 total moves.
